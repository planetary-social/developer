Vision

Everything starts with a vision, with seeing struggles and opportunities, problems and potential solutions. The act of seeing the light amidst the darkness, and duty in the times of need, these are the inspirations needed to create brave new solutions for the nasty old troubles. This is how we see the path toward the new, yet familiar socal media platform...

Roadmap

Once you see, it becomes apparent what to do. But the road from seeing, through doing, up until experiencing live and flourishing results, twisted and forked is this one. It is important to know what to do and when to act, otherwise there is a high risk of going astray, and ending up in the dark woods, or meters away from the tall unforseen cliff. This is the road taken so far, and the ones we could or should take next...

Technologies

Aren't we all magpies collecting shiny new tech, inventing new and newer ways of stitching and intertwining them together? "Keep it Simple" old saying says, "Less is More" repeats ad-infinitum in the back of our heads. Deep down however, we all now it is not the matter of volume or mass, but the overall balance that matters the most. Keeping the technological stack as simple as possible, using no more tools than necessary, and having no less undestanding of them than needed - these are the guiding forces that influence our technological decisions...
